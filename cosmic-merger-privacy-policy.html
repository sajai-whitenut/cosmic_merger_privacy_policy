Privacy Policy

Welcome to Cosmic Merger. Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play our game. By downloading or playing Cosmic Merger, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

🛰️
1. Information We Collect
Personal Data

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or account credentials. You can play Cosmic Merger without creating an account or providing any personal details.

Device & Usage Data

Our third-party advertising partner (Google AdMob) may automatically collect certain non-personal technical information, including:

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID) for ad personalization
  • Approximate location (country/region level, derived from IP address)
  • App interaction data for ad frequency and relevance
  • Network connection type
📡
2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Cosmic Merger uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID and other non-personal data to show you relevant ads.

Google's use of advertising data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time by visiting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

When personalized ads are disabled, you will still see ads, but they will not be tailored based on your interests.

👨‍👩‍👧
3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)
All Ages

Cosmic Merger is designed for players of all ages, including children under 13. We take children's privacy very seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • The game does not require registration or account creation.
  • Ads displayed to children are non-personalized (interest-based targeting is disabled for child-directed experiences).
  • We do not share children's data with third parties for marketing purposes.

If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove such information.

🔗
4. How Information Is Used

The limited technical data collected by AdMob is used solely to:

  • Display advertisements within the game
  • Prevent ad fraud and ensure ad integrity
  • Measure ad performance (impressions, clicks)
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use any data for profiling, selling to marketers, or any purpose beyond what is described here.

🤝
5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. The only third party involved in data processing is Google AdMob, which operates under its own privacy policy.

We may disclose information only if required by law, such as in response to a valid legal request or to protect the safety of our users.

🔒
6. Data Security

Since Cosmic Merger does not collect or store personal data on our own servers, there is minimal security risk from our side. Ad-related data collected by Google is protected under Google's security standards and infrastructure.

We encourage you to review Google's security practices at safety.google.

  • EU/EEA residents (GDPR): You have rights of access, rectification, erasure, and objection
